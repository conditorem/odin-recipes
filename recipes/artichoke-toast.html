Spinach Artichoke Chicken Texas Toast

Description

The “Spinach Artichoke Chicken Texas Toast” is a quick, comforting
weeknight dinner that transforms standard garlic toast into a hearty, open-
faced meal. It takes under 30 minutes to prepare and features baked Texas
toast topped with shredded chicken and a creamy, bubbly dip.

Ingredients

  • 8 slices frozen Texas toast

  • 1 1/2 cups prepared spinach artichoke dip

  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese (optional)

  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Place toast on a
    parchment paper lined baking sheet.

  2. Bake in the preheated oven for 12 minutes.

  3. Flip each piece of toast; use a spoon to press the center of each piece
    down to form a well inside the crust.

  4. Divide chicken among toast slices. Warm spinach artichoke dip in the
    microwave for 1 minute. Stir in mozzarella cheese, and top each
    toast evenly with dip.

  5. Bake in the preheated oven until heated through, 8 to 10 minutes. Turn
    broiler on High and continue to cook until just bubbly around
    the edges and lightly browned, 1 to 2 minutes.
